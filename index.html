FAQ

Questions agents ask before switching

Is this a CRM?

No. PropMatch AI is focused on property matching and shortlist sharing. It helps agents turn buyer requirements into curated property links quickly.

Can I use my own private inventory?

Yes. The app is designed for your own listings, off-market inventory, builder stock, resale options, and internal broker notes.

Will internal notes be visible to buyers?

No. Internal notes can help matching, but they are never shown on public buyer shortlist pages.

What happens if I downgrade?

Your data is not deleted. Plan limits apply to active properties. Extra listings can be archived and restored later when you upgrade.
